Main Building

The main building is the first thing you see when you enter the school and serves as the center of the school. The first floor houses the students' cafeteria and the principal's office, while the second floor is the main auditorium and gymnasium, where various events are held and physical education classes are held. The third floor houses seminar rooms, a library with about 70,000 books, and a suspension bridge that connects to the boys' dormitory, Gyowugwan.
